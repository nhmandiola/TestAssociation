Important Information

  1. Board of Directors include: Raymond W. Tompkins (President), Frances W. Tompkins (Vice President), Theresa Dearduff (Secretary & Treasurer).
  2. The Board will eventually expand to nine members with staggered terms.
  3. The HOA Agreement outlines responsibilities for maintenance, dues, and property rights.
  4. The Declaration includes conditions, restrictions, and land use regulations for the community.
  5. The MRTA Preservation Notice ensures legal continuity of the community's governing documents.
  6. The By-Laws detail voting rights, member responsibilities, and meeting procedures.
  7. Restrictions include rules about architectural changes, parking, pets, and property upkeep.
  8. Documents are enforceable and recorded with Volusia County.
